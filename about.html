Our Mission & History

The Best 10 Private Schools was founded in 2005 by a group of dedicated educators who wanted to provide a high-quality, personalized education to students in the Austin area. Our school has grown and thrived over the years, with a strong focus on academic excellence, character development, and community service.

Our teaching approach is centered around the idea that every child learns differently. We use a variety of methods and materials to engage students and make learning fun and interactive. Our experienced teachers are passionate about their subjects and are committed to helping each child reach their full potential.

We believe that extracurricular activities and character development are just as important as academics. Our students have access to a range of clubs, sports teams, and volunteer opportunities that help them develop important skills like teamwork, leadership, and empathy.
