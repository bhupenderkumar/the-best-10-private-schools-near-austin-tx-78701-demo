Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 2-5 and focuses on social, emotional, and cognitive development. Our experienced teachers use play-based learning and hands-on activities to prepare children for kindergarten and beyond.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students from Kindergarten to 4th grade and emphasizes core subjects like reading, writing, math, and science. Our experienced teachers use a variety of methods to engage students and make learning fun and interactive.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper programs serve students from 5th to 8th grade and focus on academic rigor, character development, and community service. Our experienced teachers use project-based learning and hands-on activities to help students develop important skills like critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving.

🔬

STEM

Our STEM program offers students the opportunity to develop important skills in science, technology, engineering, and math. We use hands-on activities and real-world projects to engage students and make learning fun and relevant.

🎨

Arts

Our Arts program offers students the opportunity to explore their creativity through music, art, drama, and dance. We use a variety of methods and materials to engage students and help them develop their talents and interests.

🏋️

Athletics

Our Athletics program offers students the opportunity to develop important skills like teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship. We offer a range of sports teams and activities for students of all ages and abilities.
